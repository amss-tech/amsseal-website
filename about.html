Who We Are

PROTECTING
ALABAMA HOMES

A&M Seal and Shield was founded on a simple belief: homeowners deserve a crawl space specialist they can actually trust — someone who shows up, does the work right, and stands behind it with a written warranty.

We're not a franchise. We're a locally owned Alabama LLC focused exclusively on crawl space encapsulation and moisture control. Every job is owner-operated, which means the person who quotes your job is the same person who installs it.

Alabama's humid climate creates some of the worst conditions for crawl spaces in the country. We've seen what uncontrolled moisture does to homes — and we've made it our mission to stop it before it becomes a $20,000 structural repair.

— The A&M Seal and Shield Team
